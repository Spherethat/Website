SPHERE REFER A FRIEND PROGRAM

Spread the word about us and get rewarded!

How does the Refer A Friend program work?

It's simple! All you do is spread the word about Sphere to people that you think would love the service.

 

Step 1: share your
referral link

Step 2: your friends
send in their phone

Step 3: you get
rewarded!

We're glad that you've used our service in the past and know firsthand just how convenient and easy it is. We need your help in letting others know about us now!

There are tons of ways you can share your referral link. Put it up on social media, such as Facebook and Twitter. Send your referral link to your friends through email. The more you share, the more you earn!

What are the rewards?

You have a few options for your rewards:

1. We can send you a cheque in the amount that you've earned through referrals
2. Your referral rewards amount can be added to the next phone you send to Sphere
3. Looking for a specific gift card? Let us know! We'll see if we can make it happen

Any questions about our referral program? Email us!

Earned your rewards and ready to cash out?

Let's get you paid! Send us an email at info@spherethat.ca and we'll arrange for getting you your reward payment.
