WHY SPHERE?

We do everything we can to ensure that sending your old devices into Sphere is a quick and easy process.

FAST PAYOUTS

We do our best to pay out to you as soon as we receive your phone. In some cases payments are made the next day after receiving it!

GET CASH

We pay out in two simple forms: PayPal and cheques. You can spend your money anywhere as soon as you receive your payment.

TOP VALUE

We constantly check the prices that we offer against competitors to ensure that you're getting the best value for your old device.

FREE SHIPPING

It's completely free to send us your device. The cost of shipping, materials, and everything else is covered by us.

SMOOTH PROCESS

We make sure that the entire process from you is as easy as possible, from start to finish.

HASSLE-FREE

You don't have to worry about finding shipping materials or paying for shipping. Everything's taken care of for you.

ANY CONDITION

Whether your device has no scratches at all or if it's been heartily used, we'll take it in!

RISK-FREE

If the value of your phone is reassessed to be below what it was originally, you can choose to have your device sent back at no cost to you.
