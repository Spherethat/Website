ABOUT US

Shouldn't it be easier to sell your old device when you're done with it? We sure think so.

Imagine if you could sell or recycle your old cell phone whenever you want, hassle free.

Sphere's on a mission to make this a reality.

We're working hard to change the way that we deal with upgrading our cell phones. Most of the time, the phones that you use still work perfectly fine by the end of your cell phone contract. So why aren't we doing anything about that? Once you go ahead and upgrade your phone, there's no reason to simply leave your old one in a drawer, or evenworse, in a landfill.

Our goal is to make this process as easy as possible for you. Our process is much easier and more efficient than selling your device on your own.

By working to provide you with free shipping and packaging materials, along with making the process fast and efficient, we strongly believe that we can change the way that we, as Canadians, deal with upgrading our devices.

Let's change the way we deal with our old devices.
